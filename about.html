About Psychic Medium Carlie

Carlie has had spiritual experiences from a young age, and has been drawn to the world of spirit and all things supernatural since a very young child. Carlie was able to hear the voices of those passed on, although this wasn't always fun.

Carlie recalls times when she could hear a mother screaming for her dead baby, and although she knew the voice was internal, it was deafeningly loud and distressing.

It wasn't always scary however. Carlie remembers hearing the happy chatter of an older woman, who was talking away in Italian, although she has no idea how she knew the woman was Italian, Carlie just knew.

The experiences continued through Carlie's teenage years, and she found that her whole family and even her step father were drawn to the unexplainable world of spirit communication. Everyone she knew had had some experience they could speak of.

Carlie then entered the police force as a police constable and her experiences with spirit tapered off as her mind became crowded with the difficult lives of others, and it wasn't until she left the police that the spirit world came edging its way back to her.

Carlie now enjoys a healthy relationship with the Other Realm, and is well acquainted with her guides and those beyond the veil. She is able to communicate effectively and pass on beautiful messages from those in spirit.

Carlie is a dedicated medium, and her harshest critic. There are no smoke and mirrors here. She likes to adopt and informal friendly meeting, where both reader and sitter can feel relaxed and at ease. Carlie uses the art of laughter to raise the vibration and reach the other side, and so tries to encourage joy and comedy into her readings.

Carlie is never satisfied unless she has given over at least some very specific information, and is well known for her detailed evidence.

If you are looking for a genuine Psychic Medium and Tarot Reader in Bedfordshire, then feel secure in the knowledge that Carlie will leave you feeling completely convinced of the existence of another life.
