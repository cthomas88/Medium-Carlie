Tarot Card Reading

Carlie uses the Rider Waite traditional tarot deck, with which she has a close bond with. She uses a huge variety of card spreads and layouts, some well known and some Carlie has designer herself.

She is able to read the cards fluently and descriptively to give you a sound and thorough understanding of the reason you are here on your path currently, and where you path is currently headed. Carlie is able to base this reading on a huge number of life areas, including love, relationships, finances and career.

Oracle Card Reading

Alongside the Tarot cards, Carlie uses Oracle Cards, a popular type of card deck that is able to clearly give answers to specific questions. Carlie may use any number of oracle card decks depending on the answers you seek.

The messages given in Oracle Card Readings are very descriptive, but also gentle and loving, there is no doom and gloom here. They give guidance and peace of mind and help steer you in the right direction.

Again Carlie uses various spreads or even a one card draw depending on your specific needs. No two readings are ever the same.

Clairvoyant Reading

Clairvoyant readings are those which use the reader's own psychic ability. Carlie used this method of psychic skill throughout her entire childhood, without ever knowing what it really was!

Carlie is able to imagine a scenario playing out, and give accurate predictions to tell you whether it will happen or not.

Here's an example, you want to know if your partner will propose to you, Carlie can picture the image of the proposal taking place, the celebrations afterwards, the feelings of joy and happiness you, the client, would feel, and then based on how strongly those images appear to her and how she feels, she can accurately predict whether the proposal will happen or not!

Mediumship Reading

This is by far the most popular of the services Carlie offers, and for good reason. Prior to your pre-booked appointment Carlie will get herself into a relaxed meditative state of mind, and raise her vibration, essential for a successful connection with the spirit world.

Carlie will then welcome you into her home, or if you're overseas will begin the reading. Carlie begins by inviting those in the spirit world who wish to communicate with you, to step forward and pass on messages, either directly from themselves or via her Spirit Guide, Adam.

These readings can be emotional and touching, yet light filled and joyous occasions, filled with laughter and love.

Spirit Communication and mediumship is a miracle that takes place before your very eyes, and is one of the most ancient arts, dating back thousands of years.
